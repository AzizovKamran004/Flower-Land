Learn About Our Company

FlowerLand has been around in the online flower shop industry since year 2020 and for 2 years we still continue to provide quality and surpassing service for our valued customers. The founder Mr. Tony Montana has more than a decade of experience in the flower arrangement industry which serves as one of the strength of FlowerLand as a company. Our aim is to be the prominent florist in Flower industry with wide range of well-designed flower arrangement that guarantee customer’s expectation, bring smile on everyone’s face and make each occasion memorable. Our mission is about meeting customers need and to provide unique-personalized flower arrangements for any occasion that can help customers enhance relationships with the important people in their lives. And to offer quality floral products at affordable prices so our customers receive the best value for their money.
